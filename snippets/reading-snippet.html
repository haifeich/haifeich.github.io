Reading

Reading is one of my favourite hobbies. I especially prefer to read novels. In reading, I can totally relax myself, meanwhile I can improve my languages. I often read books in every language I know. Here I would like to show four of those books I read in 2020.

Normal People is about a love story of a couple of teenagers. Sally Rooney is a young female Irish writer. She well describes the mental activies of this couple in their grown-up.

La Peste , we can not avoid this book in this paricular year. We almost re-live many scenes in the novel. Behind the pandemie, there are more things about the human beings need us to think about.

Le Grand Cahier , written by Agota Kristof, a Swiss-Hungarian writer who uses her second language French to write. She uses only simple words and short sentences. But you can still find her talent behind this funny and cruel story.

Ties is about a middle aged couple. The book is devided in three parts, from the past to the present, from the woman to the man. You can only understand the story until the end of the book, surprise.
